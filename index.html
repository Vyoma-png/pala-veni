The Legacy

Pāla Vēņi celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Telugu cuisine, where every dish tells a story of the Krishna-Godavari delta, royal courts, and timeless traditions.

Our Menu

Prakaraṇam

Gudikattu Mirapakayalu

గుడికట్టు మిరపకాయలు

Stuffed Green Chilies. A refined version of the classic regional Mirchi Bajji, featuring locally sourced heirloom chilies stuffed with spiced ground peanuts and tamarind.

River & Sea

Masaalā Chepala Iguru

మసాలా చేపల ఇగురు

River Fish Curry Reduction. Fresh Rohu or Katla river fish (the bounty of the Krishna) slow-cooked in a rich, concentrated gravy of mustard, fenugreek, and regional spices, served nearly dry.

Deccan Lamb

Gongura Maṃsa Kōsha

గోంగూర మాంస కోశ

Lamb in Sorrel Leaf Treasure. Tender lamb cooked until falling apart in a sharp, savory, and famous Gongura (sorrel leaf) paste. A legendary dish representing the deep, tangy flavors of Andhra/Telangana.

The Pulao

Pērlu Pūlāu

పేర్లు పులావు

The Pearl Pulao. A sophisticated vegetarian rice dish, featuring premium, aromatic rice, nuts, and edible silver leaf (Vark) that references the diamond/pearl trade routes that ran through the region.

Royal Chicken

Sāhi Kōḍi Vēpuḍu

సాహి కోడి వేపుడు

Royal Chicken Fry. Boneless chicken breast marinated overnight and pan-seared, then finished in a luxurious, bright red gravy of cashews, yogurt, and subtle rosewater, mimicking the Indo-Persian influences.

Madhyāhnaṃ

Sunnundalu Vēnuka

సున్నుండలు వేణుక

Black Gram Laddoo Delight. A melt-in-your-mouth sweet made from roasted black gram and pure ghee. A simple, wholesome, and traditional Telugu dessert that showcases purity and richness.

Signature Dishes

Rajula Palāvu

రాజుల పులావు

The Ruler's Grand Pulao. A sophisticated preparation of aromatic, aged rice, tender cuts of lamb, and traditional spices, finished with saffron and fried onions (birista). This dish is the centerpiece of the courtly feast.

Rajula Māṃsa Kōsha

రాజుల మాంస కోశ

The King's Mutton Treasure. A rustic, slow-cooked curry featuring robust, earthy flavors and bone-in mutton. The deep, dark gravy is achieved through hours of simmering with black cardamom, mace, and stone flower.

Gōdāvari Kāyala Kūra

గోదావరి కాయల కూర

Godavari Delta Vegetable Curry. A celebratory, seasonal vegetable preparation featuring root vegetables and gourds, cooked in a tangy tamarind pulusu (gravy) and jaggery, perfectly balancing the river delta's sweet and sour profile.
