# Videos Folder

Add your hero video here:

## Required:
- `hero-video.mp4` - Background video for hero section

## Recommendations:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
- Duration: 10-30 seconds (looping)
- File size: Under 10MB for fast loading
- Content: Restaurant ambiance, food preparation, or cultural scenes
- Aspect ratio: 16:9

## Tips:
- Use muted video (audio not needed)
- Ensure good lighting and quality
- Avoid fast movements for better loop
